Dear Dad,

For 20 years, you've lived in a foreign land, away from the comfort of your home and the warmth of your family. Every day, you woke up not for yourself, but for us.

You missed birthdays, festivals, and countless precious moments. Yet, you never complained. Your sacrifice ensured we never went hungry, that we received the best education, and that we had opportunities you never had.

This website is a small token of our immense gratitude. A digital monument to your years of selfless love and dedication. We hope it brings a smile to your face and shows you that every moment of hardship you endured was seen, felt, and deeply appreciated.

Thank you for being our hero, our provider, our guide. We love you more than words can express.

With all our love and respect,
Your Family
